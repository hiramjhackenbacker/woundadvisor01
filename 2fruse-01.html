Decision outcome & suggested actions
Quick nursing guidance (condensed)
  • Confirm referral checklist & first dose ideally given in hospital; ECG prior to first dose.
  • Correct severe hypovolaemia, hypotension, hypokalaemia/hyponatraemia prior to IV furosemide.
  • Doses & administration: bolus 20–80 mg over 2–5 min (neat); >80 mg diluted and given ≤4 mg/min to avoid ototoxicity. Infusion 5–30 mg/hr (max daily 1000 mg).
  • Monitor vitals before and after dose; notify medical staff if MEWS threshold reached (e.g., BP MEWS ≥2).
  • Document ECG, baseline labs, IV access, and escalation steps in clinical notes.
