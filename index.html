⬤ STRIDE assessment
S
Site & location
Step 1 of 6

Select the body region where the wound is located. This is recorded for clinical reference.

⬤ STRIDE assessment
T
Tissue type
Step 2 of 6

Identify the predominant tissue in the wound bed. If the wound has mixed tissue types, select the tissue type that most needs to be addressed (e.g. if both slough and granulation tissue are present, select slough).

⬤ STRIDE assessment
R

Step 3 of 6

Select all features observed in the peri-wound skin. You may select multiple.

⬤ STRIDE assessment

Infection
Step 4 of 6

Are local signs of wound infection present? Consider: increased pain, warmth, erythema, swelling, purulent or malodorous exudate, or failure to progress.
