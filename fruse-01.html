⛔ Stop infusion & escalate immediately if:

  • Systolic BP <90 mmHg or symptomatic hypotension; MEWS ≥ 2 on any core vital sign
  • K⁺ <2.5 mmol/L or ≥ 6.0 mmol/L
  • Creatinine rise ≥ 26.5 µmol/L within 48h, or ≥ 1.5× baseline (AKI Stage 1 or above)
  • Na⁺ <125 mmol/L or symptomatic acute hyponatraemia
  • Mg²⁺ <0.6 mmol/L
  • eGFR <20 mL/min (absolute exclusion criterion for HITH)
  • SpO₂ <92% RA or below patient's usual level with respiratory distress
  • Acute ischaemic change on ECG or new significant arrhythmia
  • Tinnitus or acute hearing impairment (infusion rate too fast — ototoxicity)
  • Reduced consciousness, acute confusion, or seizure
  • Signs of anaphylaxis, skin rash, or allergic reaction
  • Urine output <30 mL/hr for 2 consecutive hours
